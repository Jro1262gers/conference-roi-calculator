Conference + Event ROI Calculator

A pre-event planning and post-event tracking tool for demand generation and revenue leaders.

What it does

Set measurable goals before the event — contacts to collect, pre-event meetings split between net new logos and existing clients, MQL targets, and a revenue goal tied to your cost ratio — and hand them to sales 90 days out.

The performance scorecard tracks nine leading and lagging indicators across six reporting windows: post-event, 30 days, 60 days, 90 days, 6 months, and 9 months. Each metric grades in the window where it actually matures. Revenue and ROI grade at 6–9 months, reflecting a real B2B sales cycle — not a 90-day cutoff that penalizes long-cycle deals.

What's included


Flexible audience and MQL assumptions — conference type sets baseline defaults, every input is editable from your own historical data
90-day sales goals calculated automatically from show attendee count, conversion rate, and cost ratio
Adjacent micro event section with its own cost and pipeline contribution rolled into the combined scorecard
Composite event performance score updated at each reporting window
